Whole Lotta Heart

The Whole Lotta Heart project sought to understand which health indicators are most significant to your heart, mind, and even financial health. The team leveraged three datasets from Kaggle.com which reflected predictors like smoking, blood pressure, and relationship status and how they contribute to heart disease, stroke, and medical cost. This website visualizes those health indicators as well as displays the results of the team’s machine learning to predict the indicators impact to an individual’s overall health.
